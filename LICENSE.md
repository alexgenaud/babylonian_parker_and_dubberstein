# License and provenance

This repository has three layers with different standing.

## 1. The underlying data: Parker and Dubberstein (1942)

All chronological data ultimately derives from:

> Parker, Richard A., and Waldo H. Dubberstein. 1942. _Babylonian
> Chronology 626 B.C.-A.D. 45._ Studies in Ancient Oriental
> Civilization 24. Chicago: The University of Chicago Press.

The tables are calculated and compiled facts; the scan of the original
publication is distributed openly by the Institute for the Study of
Ancient Cultures (ISAC), University of Chicago. Any use of this
repository must cite Parker and Dubberstein.

## 2. The transcription this repository was forked from

The initial machine-readable transcription was created by Sean Redmond
(https://github.com/seanredmond/parker_and_dubberstein), published
without an explicit license. It is used here, substantially revised,
with credit; see the README. Should the upstream author state license
terms, they apply to whatever of the original transcription survives
here.
